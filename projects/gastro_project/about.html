Chef Attie studied Food and Beverages, and graduated with Honours, at Tourism and Catering College of Madrid. She went on to cook for the Hilton Brand and to do freelance cooking for international courses subsidised by the European Social Fund.

With a background in French and international cooking, our menu features an eclectic mix of traditional dishes and modern fusion. Our food is nutritious and healthy, but also tasty and decadent.

Come to Attelier to experience food in a wholesome and special way!
